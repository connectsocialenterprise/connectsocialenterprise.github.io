Training & Capacity Buildings

Understanding the tremendous need of capacity building and continuous handholding of the frontline grassroots practitioner, management staff, promoters, community leaders, social entrepreneurs CONNECT accomplished several training programs both for the domestic and international clients. Our services include need assessment, review of existing training architecture, module development, ToT, handholding, coaching etc.

Reviewing Microfinance Training Manuals and Conducting ToT for BRAC International, Bangladesh
  • Project Status : Completed
  • Client : BRAC International, Netherland

  • Project Title : Reviewing Microfinance Training Manuals and Conducting ToT for BRAC International, Bangladesh
  • Project Status : Completed
  • Clients : BRAC International, Netherland
  • Location : Myanmar, Tanzania, Sierra Leone
  • Country : Myanmar, Tanzania, Sierra Leone
  • Funded by : BRAC International, Netherland
  • Start Date : March 2018
  • Finish Date : November 2018

Project Brief

There is a felt need of BRAC and BI to standardize and unified these training modules keeping the context of these six countries into consideration. Additionally, a team of trainer will be trained by the consultant on the newly developed and approved training modules.

The overall goal of this assignment is “improved overall performance of microfinance programme of BI through its trained team of microfinance staff resulting to better provision of financial services to poor in six countries”.

The objective of the assignment is “to build the capacity of microfinance staff of BI through a series of training programs designed based on the training need assessment and review of existing training activity and training modules which will be delivered by a group of master trainers trained on the newly developed training modules”.
