Welcome To Connect

CONNECT Social Enterprise Development Services ("CONNECT") is a new generation professional institution registered in 2015 as a not-for-profit company under Section 8 of Companies Act 2013 based out of Hyderabad, India. “CONNECT” was incubated to address the tremendous need for sustainable social enterprise development and services in India.

Mr. Bismaya Mahapatra

Co-Founder & Director

A management graduate from XIMB, Bhubaneswar and more than 25 years of experience in the development sector. After the initial stint with PRADAN and TCS, Bismaya founded Harsha Trust, an established and successful NGO is Odisha and later Renaissance Strategic and Management Services Pvt Ltd. which works in the R&R (Resettlement & Rehabilitation) and CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) space. Bismaya continuously explores new avenues to serve society.

Mr. Raj Kumar

Director

Raj has done his PGDRM from IRMA, India. He has over 20 years of experience gained through projects in South Asia, Southeast Asia, and Africa. His focus has been on agriculture value chain development and financial inclusion. He is a Certified Commodity Professional, Multi-Commodity Exchange Project Identification, Development, and Management. Raj is on the board of CONNECT and VietED group that has business lines spreading into agri-business, inclusive finance and development advisory. Raj is also the co-founder of CONNECT's sister concerns Vasudha Good Harvest LLP and Vistaar Inclusive Growth Pvt. Ltd. He has earlier worked with Microsave, National Housing Bank, etc. in senior positions. He has travelled across the globe in accomplishing consulting assignment with various types of institutes from the Government, non-Government, multilateral agencies, banks, central banks, etc..

Ms. Rashmi Adlekha

Co-Founder & Director

Rashmi is a seasoned development professional with over 16 years of experience. After completing her engineering, she worked with PRADAN in tribal districts of Jharkhand for five years. After that, she worked with BASIX indifferent capacities especially in the areas of evaluation, studies, and institution development services. As a consultant, she has assisted several grassroots community institutions and NGOs. After co-founding CONNECT, she has accomplished several projects for BRAC, World Bank, IFAD, PRADAN, CMF, VietED, etc. She has travelled across India.

Mr. Suman Laskar

Co-Founder & Director

Started his career with PRADAN after completion of engineering degree from NIT, Nagpur. Later worked with BASIX in different capacities especially in the areas of consulting and training. After cofounding CONNECT in 2015, Suman assisted several institutions like BRAC International, World Bank, SIDBI, CMF, Harsha Trust, FES, etc. in the areas of financial inclusion and livelihood. He has travelled extensively across India, South-Asia & East Africa. His key interest is sustainable community-based business ent erprise development.

Dr. Megha Chakrabarty

Co-Founder

She completed her Ph.D. in Social Sciences from Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), Mumbai. Before joining TISS Mumbai for Ph.D., she had worked in the grassroots with several NGOs in Rajasthan. She is the recipient of Ph.D. Fellowship of London School of Economics - TISS Collaborative project and was the visiting researcher in the London School of Economics and Political Sciences, London, UK. Currently, she teaches at Banasthali Vidyapeeth University, Rajasthan after her stint with TISS, Guwahati as Asst. Professor.

Dr. Razdan Sarim Rahman

Co-Founder

Currently, he is working as Assistant Professor at Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), Guwahati Campus. He has more than 15 years of work experience in various capacities as researcher, teacher, and trainer in the development sector. He started his career with PRADAN (Professional Assistance for Development and Action) and later also worked with Sambodhi Research and Communications Pvt. Ltd as a professional researcher.

Navin T

Co-Founder

He is presently working with Institute of Livelihood Research and Training (ILRT) as a Senior Faculty. Navin is a Post Graduate and M.Phil in Political Science from Hyderabad Central University (HCU) and Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) respectively besides a Post Graduate Diploma in Management of Nongovernmental Organizations (PGDMN) from Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India (EDII).

Aswini Bhattacharjya

Co-Founder

He has been working as a professional practitioner in the Development Sector for the last 18 years. He started his career with PRADAN and later co-founded Seven Sisters Development Assistance (SeSTA) in 2011. Aswini was the Executive Director of SeSTA since inception till 2015 and well versed with the development sector is North-Eastern India. Mr. Bhattacharjya has a Masters degree in Business Administration from Gauhati University.
