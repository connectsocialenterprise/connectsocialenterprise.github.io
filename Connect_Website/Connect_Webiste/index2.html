ABOUT US

CONNECT Social Enterprise Development Services (“CONNECT”) is a new generation professional institution registered as a not-for-profit company under Section 8 of Companies Act 2013, India. "CONNECT" was incubated in 2014 to address the tremendous need for sustainable social enterprise development and services in India.

Consulting Agency for every Business

  • Provide high-quality technical assistance, social research and consulting services in the field of financial inclusion and livelihoods especially to build capacities of institutions which are working for communities.
  • Have meaningful engagement of qualified deeply committed professionals for intellectually stimulating and emotionally satisfying professional engagement.
  • Help establish community-owned sustainable social business enterprises to solve different social issues.
