Mr. Rak Kumar

Raj has done his PGDRM from IRMA, India. He has over 20 years of experience gained through projects in South Asia, Southeast Asia, and Africa. His focus has been on agriculture value chain development and financial inclusion. He is a Certified Commodity Professional, Multi-Commodity Exchange Project Identification, Development, and Management. Raj is on the board of CONNECT and VietED group that has business lines spreading into agri-business, inclusive finance and development advisory. Raj is also the co-founder of CONNECT's sister concerns Vasudha Good Harvest LLP and Vistaar Inclusive Growth Pvt. Ltd. He has earlier worked with Microsave, National Housing Bank, etc. in senior positions. He has travelled across the globe in accomplishing consulting assignment with various types of institutes from the Government, non-Government, multilateral agencies, banks, central banks, etc..

Mr. Ajit Mani

A pass out of prestigious Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Bangalore and pioneer in Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) space in India with more than three decades of experience. He has accomplished several evaluation projects with Bilateral and Multilateral Institutions, International and Indian NGOs, Government Departments. His keen interest in history makes him special amongst his circles and a keen traveler to several historical places. He is currently an Adjunct Professor (Project Management) at KIIT School of Rural Management, Bhubaneswar.

Mr. N V Ramana

He has over three decades of experience in conceptualizing, implementing and scaling up business models in complex areas of agriculture and financial inclusion. He is a Post Graduate Diploma holder in Business Management (MBA) (1981) from the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Ahmadabad, Gujarat India. He has worked with premier institutions such as Samunnity Value Chain Finance, India as an advisor, World Bank as a specialist on Agri-Value chains, for the project on increasing competitiveness in agriculture in Rajasthan, India, Indian Society of Agribusiness Professional as Chairman, BASIX as CEO, ITC Agro Tech Limited as General manager. He has supported many financial institutions in setting up and nurturing the agri-value chain financing models such as such as Sammunati Value Chain Finance, India, KBS LAB, India.

Dr. Sankar Datta

Sankar is a well-known face in the development sector with around three decades of experience in the field of institution development services. He has been a Member of Faculty in IRMA-Anand, IIM-Ahmedabad, and Azim Premji University, Bengaluru. He was the Founder-Dean of The Livelihood School (TLS), Hyderabad. He has worked as Senior Advisor, Institutional Development, BASIX, Hyderabad. He was the Member of Task Force for drafting the 12th Five Year Plan, Planning Commission of Govt. of India and Member of the Standing Committee on Poverty Alleviation of Chhattisgarh Planning Commission. He has accomplished numerous consulting and capacity building projects along with a number of publication in his credit.

Ms. Subha Laxmi

Fellow Chartered Accountant & Post Graduate Diploma in Equity Research with nearly 26 years' experience in the financial sector. Founder of Moneyshiksha, a Financial Literacy venture for empowering retail investors to manage their investments. Guest faculty-in premier institutes like IIM Bangalore, IIM Udaipur, Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and Institute of Company Secretaries of India. Freelance Columnist - Economic Times with over 500 articles written on investing capital markets and financial planning.
