About Connect:

CONNECT Social Enterprise Development Services ("CONNECT") is a new generation professional institution registered in 2015 as a not-for-profit company under Section 8 of Companies Act 2013 based out of Hyderabad, India. “CONNECT” was incubated to address the tremendous need for sustainable social enterprise development and services in India.
