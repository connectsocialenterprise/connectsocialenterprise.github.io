Institution Development Services

Understanding the need to strengthen the grassroots institutions engaged with community, CONNECT adopts an approach of NOT Working directly at the grassroots rather channelizes its efforts and focus in building the grassroots and community based institutions. CONNECT endeavours to provide institution development services to different sets of institutions like Not-for-Profit Institutions, People’s Institutions, Microfinance Institutions, Govt. Promoted Institutions & Programs. Over the years, CONNECT has gained substantial experience in helping small scale microfinance institutions and other community based institutions such as SHG federations and farmer producer organizations (FPOs) in building vision and business plan, developing and implementing systems and processes, technology facilitation to improve productivity and reduce cost of operation, fund (grant, debt, sub-debt) mobilization, risk management, capacity building of top and middle management leading to higher team level productivity. A structured institution development services starting with an organizational diagnosis and provision of handholding support is the USP of CONNECT.

Project Brief

Being a cooperative and managed by the community Shakti Mahila Sangh was struggling in taking a growth trajectory. It was important to have proper standard system, process and practices in place. CONNECT was hired to review and develop different systems and processes of the institution. It was also essential to bring in the systems being developed into practice. Hence a handholding assignment was taken up to develop the systems and also ensuring that the systems are being practiced.

Support KJKS in building agriculture as business enterprise in Purba Medinipur
  • Project Status : Ongoing
  • Client : Kajla Janakalyan Samity (KJKS)

Details Click to View

  • Project Title : Support KJKS in building agriculture as business enterprise in Purba Medinipur
  • Project Status : Ongoing
  • Clients : Kajla Janakalyan Samity (KJKS)
  • Location : Purba Medinipur, West Bengal
  • Country : India
  • Funded by : Action Aid, India
  • Start Date : December 2018
  • Finish Date : March 2020

Project Brief

Kajla Janakalyan Samity (KJKS) has been working in West Bengal since last 70 years. Over the years, KJKS could bring changes in agricultural practice towards promotion of sustainable agriculture. However, a need to promote agriculture as business enterprise was felt by KJKS. The overall goal of this assignment is “enhanced agricultural income of households of three panchayats of Khejuri I, Contai I and Contain III blocks of Purba Mediniupur, where KJKS is operational through promotion of agriculture as sustainable business enterprise”. The specific objectives of the assignment are (1) to standardise agricultural package and practices contextualizing with the local conditions, (2) to build capacity of KJKS staff and farmers on agriculture, (3) to support KJKS in designing and setting up a viable Farmer Producer Organization (FPO).
