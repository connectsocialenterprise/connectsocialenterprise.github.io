  • Project Title : Financial Inclusion Women Empowerment Challenge Fund (FIWECF)
  • Project Status : Completed
  • Clients : SIDBI & Shakti Mahila Sangh (SMS)
  • Location : Jabalpur & Katni Districts.
  • Country : India
  • Funded by : SIDBI
  • Start Date : Feb 2017
  • Finish Date : Oct 2018

Project Brief

The project was intended to develop the capacity of the members of SMS, the cooperative to avail responsible financial products so as to reduce the dependency of the members on informal systems. Along with that we seek to aggregate the household inputs and farm output of SMS members and offer cost efficient and remunerative prices to the members. By using technology going cashless (reduced cash transactions) both at demand side and supply side transactions are being seen as a measurable objective for financial inclusion. We will use technology to reduce cash in the system as SMS members transact most of their major cash flows (arising out of both financial and non-financial activities) through SMS.

This is envisaged to enhance the overall as well as financial decision making capacity of women & thereby increasing their Self Confidence & Self Respect & also increase the access to wider range of financial services. The project also would offer a single window, the Combo Store for all financial & non-financial transactions enabling rural households to have all transactions to buy & sell through one-point facility close to their places. Under this, the project will also digitize & present market information on real time basis to the consumers/producers.
